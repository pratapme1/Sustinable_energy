# Cero Hero: University Pilot Product Document

## 📋 **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

### **Product Vision**
Cero Hero is a comprehensive sustainability engagement platform that drives measurable behavior change through gamified carbon tracking, verified environmental activities, and community-based incentives. Our university pilot validates technology and user engagement for expansion to enterprise and municipal markets worth $7B+ combined.

### **Core Innovation**
**Dual-tier engagement system** combining:
- **Verified Activities** (10-100 credits): High-impact, measurable actions with concrete proof
- **Sustainability Insights** (1-5 credits): Daily awareness-building and habit formation

### **Market Strategy**
Use **free university pilot** as proof-of-concept to validate technology and behavior change, then monetize through:
- **Enterprise B2B** ($5B TAM): Corporate employee sustainability programs
- **Municipal contracts** ($2B TAM): City citizen engagement initiatives  
- **Consumer B2C** ($500M TAM): Individual carbon tracking subscriptions

### **Investment Thesis**
University pilot proves sustainable unit economics, technology scalability, and behavior change efficacy - validating platform for venture investment and massive market expansion.
