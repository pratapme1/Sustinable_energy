# Daily Sustainability Insights: Low-Credit Engagement Activities

## 💡 **SUSTAINABILITY INSIGHTS FRAMEWORK**
### *1-5 credit self-reported actions that build habits and awareness*

---

## 🧠 **KNOWLEDGE & AWARENESS INSIGHTS** (1-3 Credits)

### **📚 Learning & Education**

#### **Climate News Check-in** (1 Credit)
- **Action**: Read one climate/sustainability news article daily
- **Self-Report**: Share one interesting fact learned in community feed
- **Frequency**: Max 1x per day
- **Purpose**: Stay informed on environmental issues and solutions

#### **Carbon Footprint Reflection** (2 Credits)
- **Action**: Reflect on personal daily carbon impact and identify improvement area
- **Self-Report**: Log one specific behavior to modify tomorrow
- **Frequency**: Max 1x per day  
- **Purpose**: Build awareness of personal environmental impact

#### **Sustainability Tip Sharing** (3 Credits)
- **Action**: Share original sustainability tip or hack with community
- **Self-Report**: Post actionable tip in app community with personal experience
- **Frequency**: Max 1x per day
- **Purpose**: Peer education and community knowledge building

#### **Environmental Impact Calculator** (2 Credits)
- **Action**: Calculate environmental impact of daily choices (food, transport, purchases)
- **Self-Report**: Share calculation results and insights gained
- **Frequency**: Max 2x per day
- **Purpose**: Quantify personal decision impacts

#### **Sustainable Brand Research** (3 Credits)
- **Action**: Research sustainability practices of brands you use
- **Self-Report**: Share findings about one brand's environmental impact
- **Frequency**: Max 1x per day
- **Purpose**: Informed consumer decision-making

## 🏠 **PERSONAL HABITS & LIFESTYLE** (1-4 Credits)

- **Self-Report**: Log one device optimization action taken
- **Frequency**: Max 1x per day
- **Purpose**: Build energy awareness habits

#### **Natural Light Preference** (1 Credit)

#### **Standby Power Mindfulness** (3 Credits)

- **Frequency**: Max 2x per day
- **Purpose**: Eliminate unnecessary energy consumption

### **💧 Water Mindfulness**

#### **Water Usage Awareness** (2 Credits)

- **Frequency**: Max 2x per day

#### **Greywater Consciousness** (3 Credits)
