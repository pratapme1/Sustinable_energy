# Intake Note

## Project Name
Waste-to-Energy: Wet Waste & Agricultural Waste → Fuel/Energy

## Date
2026-04-02

## Sponsor / Decision Owner
Vi (Founder)

## Hypothesis
Energy, Water, Food, and Air are the four elements every human requires for as long as humans exist.
Humans, by their nature, are constant waste generators.
Focusing on wet waste and agricultural waste — both guaranteed, infinite, and currently treated as liabilities —
this project investigates the opportunity to convert them into energy (fuel), turning a permanent problem into a permanent resource.

## Primary Decision This Research Must Support
Should Vi build and pitch a waste-to-energy business targeting wet waste and agricultural waste in India,
and if so: what is the model, the opportunity size, the technology path, and the go-to-market?
