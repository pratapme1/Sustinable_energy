# Stakeholder Map

## Decision Owner
| Role | Name | What they decide |
|---|---|---|
| Founder | Vi | Build or don't build; which model; which technology |

## Primary Audiences
| Stakeholder | Type | Key Concern | How Research Serves Them |
|---|---|---|---|
| Impact Investors | External | ROI + ESG impact | Market size, unit economics, scalability |
| Venture Capital | External | Growth trajectory, defensibility | Market gap, competitive moat, TAM |
| Government (MNRE, MoEFCC) | External | Policy alignment, scale of waste problem solved | Waste reduction stats, job creation, carbon impact |
| State Governments | External | Local implementation, land, permits | State-level waste data, regulatory pathway |
| Municipal Corporations | External / Partner | Waste disposal cost reduction | Cost comparison: current vs proposed solution |
| Farmers / Agricultural Community | End User | Income from waste, cost of disposal | Revenue potential from agri waste |
| Urban Households / Citizens | End User | Clean environment, energy cost | Health impact of wet waste, energy savings |
| Technology Providers | Partner | Partnership opportunity | Technology requirements specification |
| Banks / DFIs (NABARD, SBI, etc.) | Funder | Loan repayment security | Asset-backed revenue model, subsidy support |
