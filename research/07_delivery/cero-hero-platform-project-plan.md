## 1. THE VISION

Cero Hero is the world's first platform where **organic waste becomes verified carbon identity**.

Communities — apartments, hotels, pilgrims, farms — deposit their organic waste. A community-funded physical node converts it to clean fuel (CBG), biofertiliser, and verified carbon data. Every participant earns Cero Credits tied to real, measured impact — not estimates. Those credits build a portable sustainability identity (Cero ID) that follows individuals and institutions across cities, careers, and carbon markets.

The physical infrastructure is funded by the communities it serves, layered with government grants and debt. The founder contributes execution, relationships, and a platform — not capital.

**This is not a CBG plant with an app on top. It is a sustainability identity network where the physical plant is the trust anchor that makes every digital credit real.**

## 6. COMMUNITY FUNDING MODEL

### The Core Insight

SWM 2026 (effective 1 April 2026) mandates that every bulk generator >100 kg/day **arrange organic waste processing or pay EBWGR penalty**. Every hotel and large apartment complex in Tirupati is legally required to solve this problem **right now**. We are the solution — and we sign them before the plant exists.

Signed tipping fee agreements are **contracted recurring revenue**. IREDA lends against contracted revenue as primary debt service security (alongside SATAT CBG offtake).

**This is missing from every competitor's pitch and it's a ₹500 crore market.**

SEBI mandates that the top 1,000 listed companies in India file **BRSR (Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting)** annually from FY2022-23. From FY2024-25, BRSR Core includes assured reporting on supply chain emissions and individual-level actions.

Every hotel group, every real estate company, every retailer that operates in Tirupati and files BRSR needs **verified individual and community-level sustainability data**. That is exactly what Cero ID generates.

Cero is the **BRSR data layer for community-level Scope 4 reporting** — a category with no current solution, mandatory compliance pressure, and ~7,000 potential institutional customers in India.

## 9. CERO CREDITS — ECONOMICS AND TRUST MECHANISM

### Issuance Rules (Critical for Credibility)

- **1 Cero Credit = 1 kg CO₂e avoided** (verified, not estimated)
- Credits issued only on verified physical events: weight at drop point + collection manifest + plant intake log
- Supply is **hard-capped to actual physical throughput** — no credits without corresponding waste processed
- Credits are **non-transferable in Phase 1** (anti-gaming): earned by a specific user ID, redeemable by that user only
- Credits expire in 12 months if unredeemed (prevents hoarding and speculation)

### Who Funds the Rewards Merchants Accept

This is the trust mechanism most platforms get wrong. Cero solves it with three funding layers:

**Layer 1 — Corporate sponsorship (primary)**: Companies pay to be the "sponsor" of a credit redemption category. Example: a local supermarket chain pays ₹2 lakh/month to be the "Cero Grocery Partner" — their logo on the app, their products redeemable with credits. They get customer acquisition. We fund the reward.

**Layer 2 — B2B platform fee**: Hotels and apartments paying tipping fees also pay a ₹3,000–8,000/month platform subscription for their ESG dashboard. This revenue pool funds the rewards marketplace.

**Layer 3 — Carbon credit revenue share**: 10% of VCS carbon credit sales feeds back into the rewards pool. As the platform grows, carbon revenue grows, rewards grow.

### How India-Specific Features Are Built

| Feature | India Implementation | Why |
|---------|---------------------|-----|
| User onboarding | Mobile number + Aadhaar OTP (DigiLocker) | 95%+ of target users have Aadhaar; frictionless |
| Credit redemption payment | UPI-linked (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) | 500M+ UPI users; zero merchant integration friction |
| Community notifications | WhatsApp Business API | RWAs communicate via WhatsApp groups already |
| Waste deposit verification | QR-code printed sticker on bin + app scan | No IoT hardware in Phase 1; cost ₹50 per drop point |
| Institutional dashboard | Web portal + WhatsApp report card | Hotels/RWAs prefer WhatsApp over app login |
| Carbon data export | BRSR-formatted downloadable report | Direct regulatory value; drives institutional adoption |

### Merchant Network Bootstrapping (Day 1 Problem Solved)

Before users exist, merchants won't join. Before merchants exist, users won't join. Break this with:

1. **Month 0**: Approach 10 local businesses who already have a sustainability angle (organic food stores, eco hotels, Ayurvedic shops near TTD). Offer: zero cost, free listing in Cero app, first-mover "Cero Certified" badge.

## 10. WASTE COLLECTION LOGISTICS — THE OPERATIONAL MODEL

This is the layer that kills India CBG plants. We solve it with the Cero drop point network.

### Hub-and-Spoke with Drop Points

```
[Apartment A] → Cero drop point (50m walk) ──────┐
[Apartment B] → Cero drop point (50m walk) ───── → Collection vehicle (5T, 2 trips/day)
[Hotel C]     → Cero drop point or doorstep ─────┘        ↓
                                                     CERO NODE PLANT (within 10 km)
```

- **Drop points**: Sealed HDPE bins with QR codes; placed at apartment gates, hotel loading bays, pilgrim rest areas
- **Cost per drop point**: ₹2,000–5,000 (bin + installation + QR label)
- **100 drop points for 50 communities**: ₹2–5 lakh total — negligible
- **Collection**: Contract a local waste logistics operator in Tirupati (several exist already under SBM Urban)
- **Rate**: ₹300–500/tonne contracted logistics cost
- **100 TPD × ₹400 × 330 days = ₹1.32 crore/year** — already accounted in OPEX

### Verification at Drop Point (Weight = Credits)

- Drop point bin has QR code; user scans when depositing
- Collection vehicle has weighbridge ticket per stop
- Plant has inlet weighbridge — final verified weight
- Credits issued based on plant-confirmed intake, not user-reported deposit
- This eliminates gaming: you only earn credits for waste that is actually confirmed at the plant
